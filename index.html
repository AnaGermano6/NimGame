Game Rules

Nim is a Mathematical Strategy Games where two player remove objects from different stacks one at a time. Each Player at each move, has to remove one or more objects from the same stack.
After the player finishes his/her play it must press the button "Computer Turn" to complete turn. The player that removes the last object wins!
